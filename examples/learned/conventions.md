## Shared concepts

- Foundational, cross-cutting ML concepts — positional encoding, attention,
  embeddings, normalization, optimizers — live in the **vault-root `References/`**.
  When a collection needs one, **link** to `References/{Concept}`; do **not** create a
  collection-local `References/{Concept}.md` copy.
